About Our Organization

Markham Community Connect Association

We are a federally registered Not-for-Profit Organization. We recruit and train volunteers to plan and administer seasonal sports, cultural, recreational, and community activities for a better Markham, a better future and for our children. Welcome to join us as a MCCA member (Membership fee $10, e-transfer: mcca4npo@gmail.com, message with your name and email address), we will provide one MCCA Volunteer T-shirt to our members, thanks. For any volunteer opportunity, can contact with mcca4npo@gmail.com.
